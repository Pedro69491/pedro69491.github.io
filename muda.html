In a privileged location where the countryside and nature merge with kilometers of beaches and golden sand, golf courses, marinas and exceptional natural and historical heritage. This real estate project offers a new vision of a traditional Alentejo village, built from the ground up, with homes, villas and surrounding farms.

It comprises 50 rural plots - Quintas da Aldeia where you can build your house, in a place with complete infrastructures.

Living at MUDA RESERVE is an eco-friendly rural concept that provides a high quality of life: combines nature, countryside and beach and high standards of comfort and safety. The plots are between 4 and 6 ha with total areas of construction up to 500 m2 per Quinta plus 100m2 waterproofed and there is the option of using the concept of projects defined by different architects or your own project.

MUDA RESERVE infrastructures have: own access roads, water networks (for human consumption, agriculture and fire-fighting), electricity and telecommunications networks.

Close to the Quintas da Aldeia will be implemented an urban development - Aldeia da Muda - inspired in Alentejo architecture and tradition, which will have as support structures: church, sports fields, playground, gourmet stores, newspaper kiosk and restaurant.

MUDA RESERVE is an unique refuge to be discovered just one hour from Lisbon, with 350 hectares in harmony with nature.
