URBAN FEEL

A-TOWER is located in one of the most central, noble and secure neighbourhoods in Lisbon city - Amoreiras.

This development is an unique luxury condominium with 16 floors and 34 apartments equipped with the most advanced technologies on an infrastructures, construction and finishes level and management of the condominium. All concept and architecture of A-Tower is based to provide exclusivity, wellness, comfort and charming views.

A prime residential tower with dynamic lines and character accentuated by the broad balconies offering a splendid view towards the capital’s urban center or the Monsanto Hills, Lisbon’s largest green area. The different apartments have typologies T1, T2, T3 + 1, T4 and T5 with areas between 88 m2 and 342 m2. From the apartments you can enjoy a Lisbon overview or a beautiful sunset.

Since now you will live in a sophisticated environment.

A-Tower is an imposing feature in Lisbon’s skyline.
